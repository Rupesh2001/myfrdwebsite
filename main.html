Our Programmes

Bachlor in information management

The Bachelor of Information Management (BIM) program is a Four Years (Eight Semesters) program of Tribhuvan University offering an integrated IT and Management Courses. BIM course is envisaged to enable students to develop skill in information technology, and at the same time make them able to understand the professional expertise while they occupy decision making positions.

Bachlor in business administration

Business administration is an enormous field that incorporates various of management positions. From major corporations to independent businesses, all kinds of operation needs skilled administrators in order to achieve success. The knowledge acquired through BBA highlights on Finance, Advertising, Marketing, Legal firms, Business Management, Human resource management, Tourism Management, Leadership Dynamics etc.
